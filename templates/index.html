The Core Model

Bills that live inside paychecks

Instead of a generic calendar, NorthStar Ledger organizes every bill under the paycheck it belongs to. Each period shows a running balance — income in, bills out — so you can see exactly how much you'll have left before your next paycheck arrives.

  • ✓The current paycheck is automatically flagged and expanded
  • ✓Paycheck sections with negative balances are highlighted as warnings
  • ✓Move bills to a different bucket without changing their due date
  • ✓Mark bills paid, postponed, or moved — postponed bills don't subtract from your balance
  • ✓Recurring bills auto-generate at the start of each month

Reconcile to your real bank balance

Life is messy. A forgotten charge here, a pending transaction there — and suddenly your planner doesn't match your bank. NorthStar Ledger's reconcile tool fixes that in seconds.

  • ✓Enter your actual bank balance and a date
  • ✓NorthStar Ledger calculates the difference using only paid bills
  • ✓A Balance Adjustment entry is inserted automatically
  • ✓Your running balance snaps back to reality — no manual hunting
