Full Instructions

  1. Dashboard: Use this for quick month overview (balance, income, bills, and alerts).
  2. Bills: Add bills, set due/planned pay dates, and mark paid. Use planner to avoid negative balance.
  3. Trips/Savings: Track trip targets and contributions.
  4. Promotions: Track promo deadlines and totals to avoid missed promotional windows.
  5. Calendar: Click events to edit records directly (bills/paychecks/trips/promotions).
  6. History: Manual ledger entries, completed promotions, and paid-off loan history.
  7. Export: Download an organized Excel workbook for your records.
  8. Theme: Choose your preferred visual style.
