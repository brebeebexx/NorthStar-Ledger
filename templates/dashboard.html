Your current paycheck section has a blue Reconcile to Bank button. Enter your real bank balance and NorthStar calculates the difference using only paid bills, then inserts a correction entry automatically.

Trips / Savings
Set savings goals and track contributions over time with a progress bar. Add a bill with category "savings" to count monthly contributions toward any goal automatically.

Debt Tracker
Three tools: Promotions tracks 0% interest deadlines, Credit Cards tracks limits, and Loans tracks payoff dates. The debt calculator at the top helps plan payoff timelines.

Subscriptions
